Research Experience

  • Virginia Tech Systems & Storage Laboratory (DSSL): As a member of the Virginia Tech Systems & Storage Laboratory (DSSL), under the guidance of Dr. Ali Butt and in collaboration with Yuze Li, I completed an experiment focused on optimizing memory access in Python code. By implementing code-level optimizations, we significantly enhanced the utilization efficiency of both DRAM and XRAM. This involved adjusting the code to optimize memory allocation and access patterns, thereby fully leveraging the high bandwidth of DRAM and the low latency of XRAM, ultimately resulting in substantial performance improvements.
